What this site does

fornoreason.org is a social experiment. Visitors may voluntarily send money with no stated cause, no reward, and no obligation. All donations are final and non-refundable.

Data we collect

We collect only the information you choose to provide: an optional name or alias displayed on the live feed and leaderboard. We do not collect email addresses, create accounts, or track personal information beyond what is necessary to process payments.

Payment processing is handled by MONEI. Their privacy policy applies to all transaction data.

How we use your data

The name or alias you provide is displayed publicly on the site. If you choose to remain anonymous, nothing is displayed. We do not share, sell, or use your data for any other purpose.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data associated with your donation by contacting info@fornoreason.org
